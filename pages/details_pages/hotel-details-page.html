Accomodation

Comprising 446 rooms and suites, Habtoor Grand Beach Resort & Spa offers some of the best 5 star accommodation Dubai has to offer in an unrivalled setting. All the spacious guest rooms of our Dubai luxury hotel are either sea-facing or overlooking the landscaped gardens. Beautifully furnished, the rooms and suites feature comfortable lounge areas and superb bathrooms, creating a sense of tasteful luxury and warm Arabian style. All Grand Club Suites are lavishly proportioned and enjoy magnificent views towards the beach and marina. Each suite of our Dubai resort features a separate lounge area and capacious bathroom. The Presidential, Royal and Penthouse suites offer the ultimate 5 star Dubai accommodation. Large terraces are ideal for entertaining, with views that extend across the azure Gulf.

Conference And Banqueting

For meetings, banqueting and conference facilities in Dubai, the Resort features Al Andalus - a magnificent ballroom of over 900 square meters - ideal for company get-togethers, product launches or social gatherings and can accommodates up to 1000 guests. The ballroom is decorated in a traditional style that offers state-of-the-art audio-visual equipment and can be divided into five sections according to the conference requirements. In addition, there are seven meeting rooms, one boardroom and pre-function areas that offer ideal break-out venues and rooms for smaller groups. Tailor-made outdoor and beach functions with customized dining setup and entertainment can be arranged to accommodate up to 1200 guests. The Habtoor Grand Beach Resort is your ideal conference venue in Dubai!
