    Dive into possibly the worlds bluest waters on this thrill-seeking adventure. This awesome aquatic experience begins at the cruise ship pier with a boat ride along the picturesque south end of Grand Turk. Snorkeling at one of the best reef systems Grand Turk has to offer, youll see a myriad of colorful fish and intricate coral as you snorkel in 4 - 15 feet of water.

    Back in the boat, youll head to the white-sand beach of a private island. Here you will be introduced to your Sea Doo scooter where you will get to explore the magnificent coral reef as you glide through the water on this fun water craft. You will then head to a white sand beach of a private island where your guides will provide a Conch demonstration with tasting!

    Finish this legendary tour with a leisurely ride back to the Reef Shark Bar & Grill while jamming to the rhythms of Caribbean music. Relax in a beach chair on the longest white-sand beach on Grand Turk. Food and beverages are available for purchase (at your own expense). Return to the ship by shuttle or at your leisure with a 5- to 7-minute stroll along the beach -- a perfect end to your day in Grand Turk.

    Note

    Maximum weight is 250 lbs. Minimum age allowed is 10 years. Participants must be comfortable swimming in deep water (over your head). Snorkel gear, water and punch are provided. Wear your swimsuit under your clothing; bring a towel, sunscreen and a hat. Wear non-skid, closed-toe walking shoes. Pregnant women and guests with respiratory or heart conditions are not allowed to participate. Lockers are available for rent. The Reef Shark Beach shuttle service returns to the ship every 30 minutes, subject to availability.
